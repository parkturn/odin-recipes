Odin's Chicken Piccata

Description

Chicken Piccata is a classic Italian dish that features chicken breasts that are pounded thin and then pan-fried until golden brown. The chicken is then topped with a rich and creamy sauce made from butter, lemon juice, and capers. The sauce is thickened with flour and milk, and it's served over the chicken, along with a side of pasta or rice. Odin's Chicken Piccata is a simple recipe that is easy to make and tastes great.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup capers
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • 1/2 cup garlic
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1/2 cup oregano

Steps

  1. Pound the chicken breasts thin.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add the lemon juice, capers, parsley, garlic, onion, and oregano to the skillet.
  5. Serve the chicken over pasta or rice.
